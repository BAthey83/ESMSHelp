Charter Stage

This topic describes the Charter stage of developing an ESMS assessment.

After you have added an assessment plan, you can start the process of designing an assessment plan. There are several stages to be navigated in the development process, with transitions between the steps that may require approvals. The stages are listed in Understanding Assessment Plans. The transitions are guided by the user-defined scheme.

—Quick Links—

Tasks at this stage are to

  • enter or edit assessment plan details, at the Details
  • view read-only assessment plan information that was set during assessment plan creation, but can also be viewed at the Details
  • select assets to be assessed, at the Assets
  • select groups to work with, at the Groups
  • select planning team members, at the Pre-Assessment Team
  • transition to the next stage when done.

Transition

When entries are complete and any required approvals have been obtained (this is governed by the scheme; see Transitions in Understanding Assessment Plans), you can proceed to the next stage, Pre-Assessment Stage.
