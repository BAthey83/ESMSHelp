Pre-Assessment Stage

This topic describes the Pre-Assessment stage of designing an ESMS assessment. This is the second of six stages, to be performed after approval has been obtained to progress from the Charter stage.

—Quick Links—

Tasks at this stage are to

  • upload documents to be associated with the workflow; this is done at the Documents tab
  • prepare the Pre-Assessment Questionnaire at the Questionnaire tab
  • select assessment team members at the Assessment Team tab
  • review the assessment Questionnaire at the Questionnaire Summary tab
  • transition to the next stage when done.

Documents tab

At the Documents tab, you can upload documents pertaining to the selected assets or to the workflow process in general.

Add Document

To add a document to the list of documents associated with the assessment,

  1. Activate the Upload Files button (located above the table of documents).
  2. Browse to the file to be uploaded.
  3. Activate the upload as appropriate for the browser (for example, activating the Open button in the Chrome popup).

    Note: When you upload a document at the Pre-Assessment stage, it should also show up in the list of documents accessed via the Documents tile on the ESMS dashboard.

The document name should appear in the Name column.

Remove a Document

The ability to remove a document from the list of associated documents is subject to your permission level.

To remove a document,

  1. Go to the ESMS dashboard (activate "Enterprise SMS" on the menu bar, upper left).
  2. Activate the Documents tile.
  3. Find the document using Document Search and activate the "x" button in its row.

Questionnaire Tab

The selection of questions that are presented at the Questionnaire tab depends on various factors:

  • which pre-assessment template was selected during assessment creation
  • what assets were selected at the Charter stage
  • what groups were selected at the Charter stage
  • what pre-assessment team permissions were assigned (whether you have permission for the assets that the questions pertain to)
  • whether the questions pertain to your assigned discipline(s)

Select an asset in the box at the left. Any questions pertaining to that asset will appear at the right if (1) they pertain to your assigned discipline(s) and (2) you have permission for that asset.

Note: The loading might take a couple minutes.

For each question that appears, select an answer from the answer options. Followup questions may dynamically appear, depending on whether a given answer is designed to trigger a followup question.

"Assessment Team" Tab

The pre-assessment team was assigned at the Charter stage. Here, at the Assessment Team tab, is where the assessment team will be assigned. You can add or delete assessors and designate the role (for example, Civil or Electrical or Mechanical) for each.

Add Assessor to the Team List

To add an assessor to the assessment team,

  1. Activate the Add button. The "User Search" popup will appear.

  2. Enter search text in the Name field at the top of the popup, then activate the Search button at the right side of the field.

    Note: You will get all results where the search text appears in the user name, even if it is in the middle or at the end of the user name.

  3. If too many results appear, or if no results appear, you may want to change the search text and try again.
  4. Once the list appearing in the "User Search" popup is sufficiently filtered, select one user name from the filtered list. You can only select one name per Add activation.
  5. To finalize the selection, activate the Select button at the bottom of the popup. (Alternatively, activate Close to cancel your selection.) You will be returned to the Assessment Team tab.

The assessment team are those who will answer the questions in the assessment questionnaire.

"Allow Full Desktop" Option

Mark the checkbox in the Allow Full Desktop column if you want that assessor to be able see Full Desktop mode in the field application, instead of just Tablet mode.

Edit Assignment of Disciplines

To assign an assessor to one or more disciplines (Civil, Electrical, Structural, etc.),

  1. Activate the Manage Disciplines button in the row for the assessor. The "Manage Disciplines" popup will appear.
  2. Mark the checkbox for each desired discipline.
  3. To unmark a checked box, activate the box again.
  4. When you are satisfied with your selection, exit the "Manage Disciplines" popup. The selection will be preserved.

Remove Assessor from the Team List

To remove an assessor from the list, activate the Remove button in the corresponding line of the table at the Assessment Team tab.

Questionnaire Summary Tab

At the Questionnaire tab, you can review the full list of questions that have been answered, and edit the answers as desired.

You can filter what data appears in the table based on the column headers (Facility, Component, Type, Question and Answer) by typing text into the Filter field.

To edit the answer for a question in the Questionnaire Summary table,

  1. Activate the line in the table for the desired question. A popup for that question will appear.
  2. Select a different answer than the one marked, if desired.
  3. Answer any followup questions that appear as a result of the changed answer.
  4. Exit the popup.

Note: Edits made here will take place automatically regardless of the method used to leave the question popup, even using the "x" in the upper right.

Transition

When entries are complete and any required approvals have been obtained (this is governed by the scheme; see Transitions in Understanding Assessments), you can proceed to the next stage, Review Stage.
