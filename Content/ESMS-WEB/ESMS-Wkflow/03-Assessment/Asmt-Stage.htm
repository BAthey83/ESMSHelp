Review Stage

This topic describes the Review stage of designing an ESMS assessment. The idea is that here, a different team can check over the information before synchronizing to ESMS Desktop in preparation for assessment. This is the third of six stages, to be performed after approval has been obtained to progress from the Pre-Assessment stage. After you complete the Review stage, there will be tasks to be performed outside of ESMS Web in the following stage.

—Quick Links—

The order tasks in this stage is essential. Tasks at this stage are to

  1. Review information contained at the Charter, Assets, Documents, and Teams tabs.
  2. Make changes if needed. You will need to navigate to previous stages to do so.
  3. Transition to the next stage when done.

Review Tabs

Note that the tabs described below are all for review only. No changes to the procedure can be made at the Review stage.

  • Charter (Read-Only). At this tab, you can review but not change the charter, planned assessment date, Site, etc.
  • Assets (Read-Only). At this tab, you can review but not change what assets were selected for assessment.
  • Groups (Read-Only). At this tab, you can review but not change what groups were selected for assessment.
  • Documents (Read-Only). At this tab, you can review but not change the documents that are (($$ajf TEST this )) associated with the workflow.
  • Team (Read-Only). At this tab, you can review but not change the makeup of (1) the pre-assessment team and (2) the assessment team.

Make Changes as Needed

To make any changes, you will need to navigate to the appropriate earlier stages in the workflow.

Transition

When review and edits are complete and any required approvals have been obtained (this is governed by the scheme; see Transitions in Understanding Assessments), you must (1) synchronize, (2) perform the assessment on desktop (from field notes) or tablet, and (3) re-synchronize after the assessment before proceeding to the next stage, the Post-Assessment Stage.
