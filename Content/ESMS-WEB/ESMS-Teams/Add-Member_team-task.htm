Add Team Members

This topic explains the process of adding team members. Adding team members requires permission to administer a team.

This is what the Details of a newly created team will look like before any changes:

When you add a new team, its Team Detail content area will say "No invite links" the first time the detail is displayed.

Adding new members can be accomplished in one of two ways:

  1. Add new members using the Create Invite Link button.
  2. Add a new member by activating an established, already created link is also an option. This option is available only after Create Invite Link has been used.

Option A: Use Create Invite Link

To add team members using the Create Invite Link button, three loci of action will be required, in this order,

  • at the Teams tab,
  • in email, and
  • by invited team member(s).

At the Teams Tab

At the Teams ("Team Editor") screen,

  1. Activate the Teams tab.
  2. In the "Invite Links" section of the Team Details display, activate the Create Invite Link button.
  3. In the "Create Invite Link" popup, provide an estimate of the number of team members you intend to invite.
  4. Next, activate the Create button in the popup to create invites. (Alternatively, activate the Cancel button to cancel the operation.) A new popup, "Invite Link", will appear.
  5. In the "Invite Link" popup, activate the Copy button to select the provided invite link in order to copy it into emails.
  6. Close the "Invite Link" popup using the "x" button in the upper right-hand corner of the popup.
  7. IMPORTANT: Do *not* use CTRL-V or Paste before proceeding to the steps under In Email.

In Email

In your email (or by other means),

  1. Copy the invite link into email.
  2. Send to prospective team members, up to the number of new members specified in the Use Count field shown above.

Action by Invited Team Member(s)

An invited team member needs to take these actions:

  1. Copy the link sent in the email.
  2. Paste the link into a browser window.
  3. Press the Enter key on the keyboard. An instance of Enterprise SMS Web will open.
  4. Log in to Enterprise SMS Web. After you have done so, you should see this invitation:
  5. Activate the Accept button. After activating the Accept button, the invited team member will be brought to the Dashboard. The new team membership information will be presented at the Dashboard, showing the team name, team leader, and team members.

Option B: Use Established Invite Link

When you use the Create Invite link, but not all of the expected invite links have been sent out and/or accepted, the remaining invite link(s) will show in the Invite Links section of Team Details.

To add team members using an established invite link, three loci of action will be required, in this order,

  • at the Teams tab,
  • in email, and
  • by invited team member(s).

At the Teams Tab

To use a listed link to create an invitation,

  1. Under "Link" in the list of invite links, activate the link you want to use. A new popup, "Invite Link", will appear.
  2. In the "Invite Link" popup, activate the Copy button to select the provided invite link in order to copy it into emails.
  3. Close the "Invite Link" popup using the "X" button in the upper right-hand corner of the popup.
  4. IMPORTANT: Do *not* use CTRL-V or Paste before proceeding to the steps under In Email.

In Email

In your email (or by other means),

  1. Copy the invite link into email.
  2. Send to prospective team members.

Action by Invited Team Member(s)

An invited team member needs to take these actions:

  1. Copy the link sent in the email.
  2. Paste the link into a browser window.
  3. Press the Enter key on the keyboard. An instance of Enterprise SMS Web will open.
  4. Log in to Enterprise SMS Web. After you have done so, you should see this invitation:
  5. Activate the Accept button. After activating the Accept button, the invited team member will be brought to the Dashboard. The new team membership information will be presented at the Dashboard, showing the team name, team leader, and team members.

Delete Unneeded Established Invite Link

If, in the "Invite Links" area of the Team Details display, you have invite links listed that you don't intend to use, you can delete one or more of the links using the "X" button at the right.
