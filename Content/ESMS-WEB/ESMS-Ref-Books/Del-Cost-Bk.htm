Delete a Cost Book

This topic explains how to delete a custom cost book and its related cost records.

To delete a custom cost book,

  1. Select the Reference Books dropdown menu. Choose the Cost button.
  2. Click on the custom cost book you want to delete in the Search Cost Books menu.
  3. Select the trashcan icon just above the Search Cost Books menu.
  4.  The Confirm Action popup will appear. Click the Confirm button to delete your custom cost book. Alternatively, select Cancel to keep your custom cost book.

Delete Cost Records

To delete a cost record,

  1. Find and select a cost record to delete from the custom cost book.
  2. Click the trashcan icon under Remove to delete the cost record.

Note: If the cost record has not been updated with recent changes, the trashcan icon may not appear under Remove. Update the cost record data to ensure the trashcan icon appears when needed.
