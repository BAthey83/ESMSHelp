Delete an Inflation Book

This topic demonstrates how to delete an inflation book in Enterprise SMS, with detailed descriptions for each task.

Permissions Note: Deleting a custom inflation book requires Work Planner permission level or above at the target Organization.

To delete a custom inflation book,

  1. Select the custom inflation book you wish to delete at the Search Inflation Books window.
  2. Click the trashcan icon above the Search Inflation Books window to begin the deleting process.
  3. The Confirm Action popup window will appear. Click the Yes button to confirm the deletion.
