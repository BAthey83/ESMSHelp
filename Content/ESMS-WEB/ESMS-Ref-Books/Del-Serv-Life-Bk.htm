Delete a Service Life Book

This topic explains to how to delete a custom service life book. The original Reference service life book cannot be deleted.

Permissions Note: Deleting a service life book requires Master Planner permission level or above at the target Organization.

To delete a service life book,

  1. Select a service life book under the Service Life search window.
  2. Click the Delete Book icon, which is the trashcan icon next to the Add Book icon (the "+" sign).
  3. The Confirm Action window will appear. Confirm the deletion by selecting Yes.
