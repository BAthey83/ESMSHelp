Edit a Service Life Book

This topic covers how to add or edit data in a custom service life book. If the custom book still needs to be created, the topic Add a Service Life Book explains how.

Permissions Note: Editing a custom service life book requires Master Planner permission level or above at the target Organization.

 

—Quick Links—

  • Open a Custom Service Life Book
  • Edit Data
  • Save Changes

Open a Custom Service Life Book

To open a custom service life book,

  1. Choose an Organization with the Current Organization button.
  1. Select the Reference Books drop-down on the main menu, then choose the Service Life button when the menu expands.
  2. Service Life book options will appear under the Search SL Books search window. Make a selection from the available options, or create a new custom Service Life Book. Refer to Add a Service Life Book for more information on this process.

Edit Data

Once you have selected a Service Life Book, the Specification search results appear. If you wish to narrow the scope of the search results, you can type in a phrase (ex. "Area") into the Specification search box.

In the service life book results, you can edit the data for the following fields:

  • Service Life
  • Paint Life

Note: If you wish to clear your edits from the service book, click the Remove icon (the trashcan button at the far right).

Save Changes

Once you have finished editing your Service Life Book, select the Save icon highlighted above to retain your updates.
