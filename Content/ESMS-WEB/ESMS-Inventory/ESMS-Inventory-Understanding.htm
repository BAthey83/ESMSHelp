What Can I Do Here?

The Inventory screen is where you search for organizations, facilities/assets, sites,groups, and components, applying filters if desired and narrowing your search to applicable sites and domains. You can also configure your data by selecting the information fields you need.

Note: Before you begin a search, you must select at least one domain from the main menu bar dropdown. Selecting a site is optional (all sites permitted to you are active if you don't select a site).

Once the results appear onscreen, you can access the detail pages for the resulting organizations, sites, facilities/assets, groups, or components.

Alternatively, you can begin at the starting page (which shows facilities/assets by default) and navigate down to the desired information.

Inventory Screen Tasks

At the Inventory screen, you can perform the following tasks:

  • search organizations
  • search sites
  • search facilities/assets
  • search groups
  • search components

Inventory Screen Information

You can access information via the Inventory screen and the corresponding detail pages:

Organization Details

At the Organization Details page you can see:

  • a list of organizations
  • a list of sites related to the organization
  • a list of performance records
  • a list of FCIs by domain
  • a list of funding sources by year
  • a list of organization members and their related permissions
  • a list of reference books related to the organization

Site Details

At the Site Details page you can see:

  • a list of sites and their related data
  • maps for the associated sites
  • graphs with key site and system data
  • a list of site members and their related permissions

Facility/Asset Details

At the Facility Details page you can see:

  • a list of components in the facility/asset
  • a list of assessments for components in the facility/asset
  • a list of work items for components in the facility/asset
  • a list of documents for components in the facility/asset
  • a list of cost modifier books for components in the facility/asset
  • a list of performance records

Group Details

At the Group Details page you can see:

  • what components are in the group
  • what child groups are in the group
  • group associations
  • a list of assessment plans for all components contained in the group or its child groups
  • a list of work items for all components contained in the group or its child groups

Component Details

At the Component Details page you can see:

  • component attributes
  • a list of component details
  • building section properties
  • a list of assessment plans performed on the component
  • a list of work items associated with the component
  • the component's inspection schedule
  • a list of child components
  • associated documents, including images
  • a list of cost modifiers
  • a list of performance records

As mentioned at Inventory Screen Tasks, you can also upload documents, which will be automatically associated with that component.
