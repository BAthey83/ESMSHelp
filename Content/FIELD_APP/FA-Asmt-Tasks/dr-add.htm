Add Direct Rating

To add a direct rating,

  1. At the Systems tab in the navigation pane, navigate to and select the component to be rated.
  2. At the assessment plans tab, activate Direct Rating in the ribbon. A Direct Rating tab will appear in the content pane.
  3. In the content pane toolbar, activate the Manage Direct Rating Sets button.
  4. In the "Direct Rating" popup, activate Add New.
  5. Select the radio button next to the desired color rating (RedMinus, Red, Amber, Green, GreenPlus or "No Rating").
  6. QQ Activate Manage Direct Rating Sets again.
  7. In the "Direct Rating" popup, activate Close.
