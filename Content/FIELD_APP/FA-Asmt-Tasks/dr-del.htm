Delete Direct Rating

To delete a direct rating,

  1. At the Systems tab in the navigation pane, navigate to and select the component you wish to delete a direct rating from.
  2. At the assessment plans tab, activate Direct Rating in the ribbon. A Direct Rating tab will appear in the content pane.
  3. Activate the dropdown arrow in the content pane toolbar.
  4. The dropdown list will show dates and inspector ("Created by") for the direct assessment plans you have performed on the selected component.

  5. Activate the Manage Direct Rating Sets button.
  6. In the "Direct Rating" popup, activate the dropdown arrow in the "Direct Rating List" field, and select the desired rating by its date. (A triangle pointing right shows which of the ratings is currently selected.)
  7. After you make your selection, the desired rating date and time should be displayed in the "Direct Rating List" field.
  8. Activate the Delete button at the bottom of the popup.
  9. Lastly, activate the Close button to close the popup.
