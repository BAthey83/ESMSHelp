Edit System

Edits/changes you can make to a system are:

  • Change the system's importance factor
  • Change the system's name ($$Pending functionality)

Importance Factor

To change a system's importance factor,

  1. At the Tools tab, activate Group Editor in the ribbon. A Group Editor tab will open in the content pane.
  2. In the list of group types (located at the left side of the content pane), activate "System". A listing of all systems will appear to the right, along with the importance factor percentage for each.

  3. In the column labeled "Importance Factor (%)" edit the number for the importance factor of the new system. This number is a percentage, where the total of all the numbers in the "Importance Factor (%)" column should add to 100.
  4. Next, adjust one or more numbers in other rows to compensate for the change you just made in the previous step, such that the total of all the numbers in the "Importance Factor (%)" column add to exactly 100.
  5. IMPORTANT: Before exiting, activate the Save Systems button.
