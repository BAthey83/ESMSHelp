View Group

See Groups Being Used

To see what groups are currently in use, having member components,

  1. Add the Groups tab to the navigation pane if it's not already there (to do so, activate Groups in the ribbon at the Home tab).
  2. In the navigation pane, select the Groups tab.
  3. Expand the list. A quick way to show group-types and groups, as well as their member assets, is to use the (Expand All) button. Alternatively, click the arrow to the left of the site name to show the list of group-types in use; then click the arrow left of the desired group-type to view its groups.

To see what components are in a particular group,

  1. Follow the steps in See Groups Being Used.
  2. To find the desired group, either (a) browse the expanded list or (b) use the search bar. The search bar is located immediately below the Expand All and Contract All buttons.
  3. Members of the group will be displayed in an indented list below the group name.

See Available Groups

To see all groups that are available,

  1. At the Tools tab, activate Group Editor in the ribbon. A Group Editor tab will appear in the content pane, listing all group types in the left sub-pane.
  2. Above the list of group types, activate the Domains button to select applicable domains. By default, all domains are active in the Group Editor.
  3. Activate a group type to see a listing of its groups in the right sub-pane.
