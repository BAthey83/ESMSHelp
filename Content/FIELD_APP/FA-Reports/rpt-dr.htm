Direct Rating Summary Report

Generate Report

To generate a direct rating summary report,

  1. Activate Direct Rating Summary in the ribbon at the Reports tab.
  2. In the Parameters pane on the left side of the print preview popup, activate the dropdown arrow at the right of the "Direct Rating" field.

    Troubleshooting Tip: If the Parameters pane is not showing, select Parameters in the ribbon at the popup's Preview tab.

  3. Select one of the dropdown options.
  4. Activate the Submit button.
  5. To see a different summary, expand the dropdown, select another direct rating from it, and activate Submit.
  6. Repeat the step above as desired.

Save Report

To save a report,

  1. While the report is showing in the print preview popup, activate Save in the ribbon at the Preview tab. (Alternatively, activate Save in the dropdown at the leftmost tab.)
  2. In the "Save As" popup, navigate to the desired save location.
  3. Give the file a more specific identifying name to replace .
  4. Activate the Save button (at the lower right of the "Save As" popup).

Print Report

To initiate a print of the report from the print preview popup, choose one of the following:

  1. Activate Print in the ribbon at the popup's leftmost tab, OR
  2. Activate Print in the in the dropdown at the leftmost tab.
