    Edit Direct Rating

    To edit a direct rating,

    1. At the Systems tab in the navigation pane, navigate to and select the component whose rating you wish to edit.
      2. >>>>>>> master
    2. At the assessment plans tab, activate Direct Rating in the ribbon. A Direct Rating tab will appear in the content pane.
    3. To select the exact rating you wish to edit (there may be multiple ratings, with different dates), activate the dropdown arrow in the content pane toolbar. The dropdown list will show dates and inspector ("Created by") for the direct assessment plans you have performed on the selected component.

    4. Select the desired rating by its date. (A triangle pointing right shows which of the ratings is currently selected.)
    5. To change the color rating, select the radio button next to the desired color rating (RedMinus, Red, Amber, Green, GreenPlus or "No Rating").
    6. QQ Activate Manage Direct Rating Sets.
    7. In the "Direct Rating" popup, activate Close.
