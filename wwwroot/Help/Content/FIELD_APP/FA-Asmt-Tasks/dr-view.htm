    View Ratings for a Component

    To view direct ratings you've created for a component,

    1. At the Systems tab in the navigation pane, navigate to and select the desired component.
      2. >>>>>>> master
    2. At the assessment plans tab, activate Direct Rating in the ribbon. A Direct Rating tab will appear in the content pane.
    3. In the content pane toolbar, activate the dropdown arrow next to the date of the most recent direct rating for the selected component. The dropdown list will show dates and inspector ("Created by") for the direct assessment plans you have performed on the selected component.

    View Ratings for Multiple Components

    =======

    View Ratings for Multiple Components

    >>>>>>> master

    Once you're at the Direct Rating tab in the content pane

    1. At the assessment plans tab, activate Direct Rating in the ribbon. A Direct Rating tab will appear in the content pane.
    2. At the Systems tab in the navigation pane, navigate to and select each desired component, viewing the rating for each as you go.

    Ratings by Other Inspectors

    To view date and inspector information for all direct ratings,

    1. At the Systems tab in the navigation pane (from any top tab), navigate to and select a component.
    2. In the toolbar, activate the dropdown arrow next to the date of the most recent direct rating for that component. The dropdown list will show date and inspector ("Created by") for all of the direct assessment plans performed on the selected component.
