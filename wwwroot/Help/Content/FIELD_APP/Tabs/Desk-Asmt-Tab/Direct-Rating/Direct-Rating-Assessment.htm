Direct Rating in the asset tree

If the "Direct Rating" checkbox appears above the asset tree, you can perform a direct rating as follows:

  1. Direct Rating Checkbox. Mark the "Direct Rating" checkbox located above the asset tree.

  2. Select Inspector Name. To the right of the checkbox is a field for the inspector name.
    1. If your name is already in the field, ignore this step.
    2. Otherwise, activate the down arrow located at the right end of the field (see red box below) and then select your inspector name from the dropdown list.
