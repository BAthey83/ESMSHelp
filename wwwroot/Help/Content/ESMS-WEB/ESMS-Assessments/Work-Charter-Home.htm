Charter Stage

This topic describes the Charter stage of developing an ESMS assessment.

After you have Add Assessment Plans, you can start the process of designing an assessment plan. There are five stages to be navigated in the development process, with transitions between the steps that may require approvals. The stages are listed in Understanding Assessment Plans. The transitions are guided by the user-defined Scheme.

—Quick Links—

Tasks at this stage are to

  • enter or edit assessment plan details, at the Details Tab
  • view read-only assessment plan information that was set during assessment plan creation, but can also be viewed at the Details Tab
  • select assets to be assessed, at the Assets Tab
  • select groups to work with, at the Groups Tab
  • select planning team members, at the Operator Pre-Assessment Team Tab
  • Transition to the next stage when done.

Transition

When entries are complete and any required approvals have been obtained (this is governed by the scheme; see Transitions in Understanding Assessment Plans), you can proceed to the next stage, Pre-Assessment stage.
