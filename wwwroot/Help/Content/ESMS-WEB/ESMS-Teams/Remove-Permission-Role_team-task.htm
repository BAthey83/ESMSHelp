Remove a Permissions Role

This topic explains the process of removing a permission role that has been assigned to a team as a whole. This task requires that you have permission to administer a team.

Removing a permissions role is done in the "Team Workspace" area at the Teams tab.

Remove Site Role

To remove a site role,

  1. Navigate to the Teams tab on the Teams screen.
  2. Select the desired team. The Team Details should appear in the content area to the right.
  3. Make sure the Sites tab is active in the Team Workspace area.
  4. Activate the "x" button to the right of the permissions target/role combination you want to remove.

Remove Group Role

To remove a group role,

  1. Navigate to the Teams tab on the Teams screen.
  2. Select the desired team. The Team Details should appear in the content area to the right.
  3. Activate the Groups tab in the Team Workspace area.
  4. Activate the "x" button to the right of the permissions target/role combination you want to remove.
