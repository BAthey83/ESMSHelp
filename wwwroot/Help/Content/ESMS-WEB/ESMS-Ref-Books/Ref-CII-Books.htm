Accessing CII Books

To access CII Books,

  1. Open the Reference Books drop-down menu.
  2. Select the Component Importance button, as highlighted above.

Now, you can select a CII book.

About CII Books

The Component Importance Index, or CII, is used to rank the relative importance of Components against one another. The CII is commonly utilized in features such as work configurations where certain Components will need to be held to a much higher standard than other Components, or in budgeting, where certain Components should be funded before other Components.

Custom CII Component Importance Index books can be created to customize the Component Importance Index ratings for specific Components unique to your Organization. For example, your Organization may have different standards than other Organizations, or certain Components may be mission-critical to your Organization but not others. Each CII book is associated with an Organization and will affect all Components within that Organization.

 

Selecting CII Books

To select CII books, go to the Search window. In addition to selecting CII books, you can Add a CII book by clicking the plus icon on the left, Delete a CII book by clicking the trashcan icon in the middle, or Edit the Book Name by selecting the pencil-and-paper icon on the right.

Fields can be customized as needed. To add or remove fields, click the Select Columns button, as indicated by the triple-dash icon highlighted above.

The Select Columns to View popup will appear in a new window. At the Select Columns to View popup, you can choose as few or as many fields as you need for your CII book. Once you have made your selections, click the Update button to save them, or click Cancel to discard them.

In addition, you can perform the following options after you have made edits to your CII book:

  • Save Changes: Select the floppy disk icon on the left to retain your edits to an existing CII book.
  • Filter: Select the center icon to add a filter to your CII book search results.
  • Select Columns: Choose the triple-dash icon on the right to select which data columns to view in your CII book.

 

The following fields can be edited on this page:

  • Specification: The item selected for editing within the CII book.
  • Type: A generalized item classification compared to the Specification. For example, a Specification may be "Pipe, Copper, 8-12 Inch." The Type will simply be "Pipe."
  • Domain: The domain applied to the work action, such as Utility-Gas, Waterfront, or Facility-Buildings.
  • Importance: The ranking of the Specification within the CII book.

You can select Filters by each field column () to narrow or expand your search. Results can be organized in ascending or descending order by selecting the minus icon by each field column. Click the minus icon once to get the Up caret, which outputs results in ascending order, click it twice to get the Down caret, which outputs results in descending order, or click it three times to get the minus icon again, which will revert the CII books back to their original order.

If a CII book has been overridden or updated, you will see the Remove button (the trashcan icon) at the far right of the item row. Select this button to delete a CII book.
