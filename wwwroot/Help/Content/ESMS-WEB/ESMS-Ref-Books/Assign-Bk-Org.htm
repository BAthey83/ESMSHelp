Assign a Book to an Organization

This topic describes how to assign a book to an Organization.

Note: The following steps can be applied to all Reference Books, including Cost Books, CII Component Importance Index Books, Service Life Books, and Inflation Books.

To assign a Book to an Organization,

  1. Select the Inventory drop-down menu and click Organizations.
  2. Choose an Organization you wish to assign a book to from the Search Organizations screen.
  3. In the subsequent menu, scroll to the Reference Books tab. The Organization Reference Books screen will appear.
  4. Select one or multiple Reference Book(s) available for your Organization from the dropdown menu(s).
  5. Once you have made your choice(s), click Save, which is located at the bottom of the page.
