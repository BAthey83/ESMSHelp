Delete Group

To delete a group,

  1. Select the Tools tab.
  2. Open the Group Editor from the ribbon.
  3. At the Group Editor tab, select the group type containing the group you wish to delete (see the red "1" in the screenshot). The selected group type will be indicated by a right-facing triangle in the leftmost column of the content pane.

  4. In the listing of groups under "Name" select the group you wish to delete (see the red "2" in the screenshot).
  5. Activate the Delete Group button (see the red "3" in the screenshot).
  6. In the "Delete Alert" popup, activate Yes.
