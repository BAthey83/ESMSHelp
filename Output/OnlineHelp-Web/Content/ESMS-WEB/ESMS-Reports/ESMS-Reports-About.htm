Understanding the Reports Screen

This topic introduces tasks you can perform at the Reports screen and provides links to greater detail for each. It also provides an overview of navigating the Reports screen.

What Can I Do Here?

As of July 2019, no reports have been adapted for use with Enterprise SMS Web. After this takes place, you will be able to select from among report types that are tailored for the different domains in Enterprise SMS, then generate and view the report.

Reports Tasks

At the Reports screen you can

  • (Optional) narrow the list of available reports to those tailored for just one of the Enterprise SMS domains
  • select a report to be generated
  • view the report

Navigating the Reports Screen

At the Reports screen, selecting the domain is carried out in the left column. "All" domains is also a valid selection.

Selecting a report to be generated is carried out in the right column.

Once these selections have been made, the View Report button near the bottom of the screen can be activated to generate the report.
