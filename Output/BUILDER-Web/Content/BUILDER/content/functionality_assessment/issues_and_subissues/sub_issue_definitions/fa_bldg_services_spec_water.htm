Building Services - Specialty Water Supply

Functionality Issue: Building (or Functional Area) Services

Sub-Issue: Specialty Water Supply

Definition:

The specialty water supply in the building (or functional area) is inadequate.

Severity Levels:

Red - There is no specialty water supply or the water supply is inadequate, posing a health problem or adversely affecting the mission to a significant degree.

Amber - The specialty water supply is inadequate, but health is not a problem and the mission is not affected to the level of red.

Green (+) - The specialty water supply is adequate.

Examples:

  • The water in the building (or functional area) is not deionized.
  • The water in the building (or functional area) is not distilled.
