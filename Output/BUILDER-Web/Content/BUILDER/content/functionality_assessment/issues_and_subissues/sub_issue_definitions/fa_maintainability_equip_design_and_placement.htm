Maintainability - Design and Placement of Equipment

Functionality Issue: Maintainability

Sub-Issue: Design and Placement of Equipment

Definition:

The design and/or placement of equipment in the building (or functional area) does not allow for easy maintenance.

Severity Levels:

Amber - The design and/or placement of equipment does not allow for easy maintenance, resulting in insufficient care.

Green (+) - The design and placement of equipment allows for easy maintenance.

Density:

Automatically computed by BUILDER.

Examples:

  • The transformer is directly next to a fence, making it difficult to maintain.
  • Deficient sub-components on an air handler cannot be reached to repair or replace.
