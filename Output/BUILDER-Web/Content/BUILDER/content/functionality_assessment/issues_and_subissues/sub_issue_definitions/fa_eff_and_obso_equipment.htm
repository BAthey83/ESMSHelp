Efficiency - Equipment

Functionality Issue: Efficiency and Obsolescence

Sub-Issue: Equipment

Definition:

The equipment in the building (or functional area) is inefficient and/or obsolete.

Severity Levels:

Amber - The equipment’s technology is ineffective, inefficient, and/or unreliable.

Green (+) - The equipment’s technology is effective, efficient, and reliable.

Density:

Automatically computed by BUILDER.

Examples:

  • The water pump is energy inefficient.
  • The boiler cannot be repaired due to obsolescence (i.e. cannot get repair parts).
