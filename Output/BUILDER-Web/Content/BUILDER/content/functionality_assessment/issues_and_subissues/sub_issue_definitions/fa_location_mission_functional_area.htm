Location - Mission Support (Functional Area)

Functionality Issue: Location

Sub-Issue: Functional Area Location

Definition:

The functional areaâ€™s location is inadequate to support the mission.

Note: This sub-issue only appears in the second tier of the functionality assessment.

Severity Levels:

Red - The functional area is located inadequately for support, adversely affecting the mission to a significant degree.

Amber - The functional area is located inadequately for support, but the mission is not affected to the level of red.

Green (+) - The functional area is located adequately for support of the mission.

Examples:

  • The administration area is not located near the conference rooms (assembly areas).
  • The restrooms are not located centrally to the building.
  • The security area is located in or near public areas of the building.
