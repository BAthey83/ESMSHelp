Environment/Health - Lead Paint

Functionality Issue: Environment/Health

Sub-Issue: Lead Paint

Definition:

Lead paint is present in the building (or functional area).

Severity Levels:

Red - Lead paint is present in the building (or functional area), posing a health problem.

Amber - Lead paint is present in the building (or functional area), but health is not a problem.

Green (+) - No lead is present in the building (or functional area).

Density:

x 100 = Problem Density

A = Affected Area of the Building (or Functional Area)

B = Total Area of the Building (or Functional Area)

Density Ranges:

  • 1% - 10%
  • 10% - 25%
  • 25% - 50%
  • 50% - 100%

Examples:

  • Lead paint is flaking off the walls in the building (or functional area).
  • Lead paint is painted over and sealed with a non-lead based paint in the building (or functional area).
