Environment/Health - Indoor Air Quality

Functionality Issue: Environment/Health

Sub-Issue:Indoor Air Quality (IAQ Indoor air quality)

Definition:

The IAQ Indoor air quality in the building (or functional area) is inadequate.

Severity Levels:

Red - The IAQ Indoor air quality in the building (or functional area) is inadequate, posing a health problem.

Amber - The IAQ Indoor air quality is inadequate (or functional area) in the building, but health is not a problem.

Green (+) - The IAQ Indoor air quality is adequate (or functional area) in the building (or functional area).

Density:

x 100 = Problem Density

A = Affected Area of the Building (or Functional Area)

B = Total Area of the Building (or Functional Area)

Density Ranges:

  • 1% - 10%
  • 10% - 25%
  • 25% - 50%
  • 50% - 100%

Examples:

  • Toxic chemicals are present in the building (or functional area).
  • Multiple users have developed occupational asthma in the building (or functional area).
