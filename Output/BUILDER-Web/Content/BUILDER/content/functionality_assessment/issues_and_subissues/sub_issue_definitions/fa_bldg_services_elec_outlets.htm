Building Services - Electrical Outlets

Functionality Issue: Building (or Functional Area) Services

Sub-Issue: Electrical Outlets

Definition:

The electrical outlets in the building (or functional area) are inadequate.

Severity Levels:

Red - Electrical outlets are inadequate, violating code, posing a life safety issue, and/or adversely affecting the mission to a significant degree.

Amber - Electrical outlets are inadequate, but codes are not violated, life safety is not an issue, and the mission is not affected to the level of red.

Green (+) - Electrical outlets are adequate.

Density:

x 100 = Problem Density

A = Number of Inadequate Electrical Outlets in the Building (or Functional Area)

B = Total Number of Electrical Outlets in the Building (or Functional Area)

Density Ranges:

  • 1% - 10%
  • 10% - 25%
  • 25% - 50%
  • 50% - 100%

Examples:

  • There are not enough electrical outlets in the building (or functional area).
  • The electrical outlets are in poor locations throughout the building (or functional area).
