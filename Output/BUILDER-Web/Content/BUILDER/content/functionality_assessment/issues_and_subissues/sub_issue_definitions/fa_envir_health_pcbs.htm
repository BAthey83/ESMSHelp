Environment/Health - Polychlorinated Biphenyls (PCBs)

Functionality Issue: Environment/Health

Sub-Issue: Polychlorinated Biphenyls (PCBs Polychlorinated biphenyls)

Definition:

PCBs Polychlorinated biphenyls are present in the building (or functional area).

Severity Levels:

Red - PCBs Polychlorinated biphenyls are present in the building (or functional area), posing a health problem or violating code.

Amber - PCBs Polychlorinated biphenyls are used in the building (or functional area), but health is not a problem and codes are not violated.

Green (+) - No PCBs Polychlorinated biphenyls are present in the building (or functional area).

Density:

x 100 = Problem Density

A = Affected Area of the Building (or Functional Area)

B = Total Area of the Building (or Functional Area)

Density Ranges:

  • 1% - 10%
  • 10% - 25%
  • 25% - 50%
  • 50% - 100%

Examples:

  • Exposed PCBs Polychlorinated biphenyls are present in the building (or functional area).
  • Encapsulated PCBs Polychlorinated biphenyls are present in the lighting fixtures in the building (or functional area).
