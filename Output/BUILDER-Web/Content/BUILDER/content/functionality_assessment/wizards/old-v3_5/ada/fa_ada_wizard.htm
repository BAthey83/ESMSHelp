Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) Wizard

When performing a first tier or second tier functionality assessment, some of the sub-issues use a wizard to determine the rating. The ADA compliance sub-issue is one of those sub-issues. To open the ADA Wizard, expand the ADA issue in the functionality assessment screen (activate the "+" next to the issue) and activate the Wizard... button in the ADA compliance sub-issue row in the grid.


The ADA Wizard will appear in a new window.


The first step in the wizard is to answer the questions regarding the ADA compliance required in your Building (or Functional Area). Answering these questions will determine if your Building (or Functional Area) is grandfathered from ADA compliance or not.

After opening the wizard, the question: "Was the building (or functional area) built after July 26, 1992?" will be shown. Answer this question and continue down the ADA Wizard answering the questions as either Compliant, Non-Compliant, or N/A. If you need help determining whether your Building meets these individual requirements, there is a plus sign to the left of the questions which further explain the necessary requirements. Activate the plus sign to get further information regarding individual questions.

After completing the ADA checklist, activate the OK button to proceed. Based on the data entered in the checklist, the color rating for the sub-issue will be shown.
