Seismic Wizard

*** This Wizard is not currently operational ***

When performing a first tier functionality assessment, some of the sub-issues use a wizard to determine the rating. The seismic loading conditions sub-issue, which is a sub-issue of the Structural Adequacy issue, is one of those sub-issues. To open the Seismic Wizard, expand the Structural Adequacy issue in the functionality assessment screen (activate the "+" next to the issue) to see the sub-issues and activate the Wizard... button in the seismic loading condition sub-issue row in the grid.


The Seismic Wizard will appear in a new window.


The data collected in the Seismic Wizard in BUILDER is formatted to match the data used by the Federal Emergency Management Agency's (FEMA Federal Emergency Management Agency) seismic survey methodology. BUILDER currently only provides a mechanism for recording an off-line seismic evaluation. The data entered here is simply recorded without any built-in analysis to determine the evaluation, i.e. you must select the Outcome of Evaluation or the Exempt category from the dropdown lists.

The Seismic color-coded box on the general building inventory screen will be red if the seismic survey indicates the building is seismically deficient, yellow if the building is exempt, and green if the building if OK.

After all of the data have been entered, activate the OK button in the toolbar to proceed. The results of the wizard will be shown in the grid. If you wish to change any of the data entered in the wizard, activate the Wizard... button again and the Seismic Wizard will reappear and can be edited.
