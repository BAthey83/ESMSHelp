Delete a Roof Section

To delete a roof section, navigate to it in the inventory tree and do one of the following:

  1. (1) Right-click the roof section, then (2) select "Delete Roof Section." Or,
  2. (1) Select the roof section, then (2) activate the Delete Roof Section icon on the menu toolbar.

WARNING: This is a significant step to take and should only be done when you are certain that you wish to clear the roof section you have selected from the inventory in the Building.

Backups: Making frequent backups of your inventory database will protect you from significant data losses if mistakes in deleting are made.
