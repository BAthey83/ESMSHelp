About Roof Sections

A roof section is the final and most detailed level in the ROOFER asset tree. This topic briefly explains by what criteria a Building's roof should be divided into different conceptual sections, and introduces some of the features of a roof section record. It also provides links to the operations that can be performed at the roof section level.

Once a Building is in place in the ROOFER database, one or more roof section records can be added to the Building. Areas of roof that differ from each other in age (including the age of replacements or added layers), type, or composition should be described in separate roof sections.

Inventory operations you can perform on Roof Sections are:

  • Add a Roof Section
  • Edit Roof Section Inventory Data
  • Delete a Roof Section
