Inventory Overview

This topic provides a brief overview of typical procedures in the initial and ongoing process of ROOFER™ inventory.

There are two primary stages in using ROOFER™:

  1. Inventory stage
  2. Maintenance stage.

Entering roofing inventory and other associated elements into ROOFER is the sole focus of the first stage. During the second stage, inventory tasks will still be performed, for example when:

  • Information entered during initial inventory needs to be revised.
  • Roof sections are repaired or replaced.
  • Additional inventory is added.

The Goal of Inventory

During the Inventory Stage

The goal of the inventory stage is to catalog a roofing portfolio in such a way as to produce, in ROOFER, a completed, hierarchical inventory tree where information about each roof section is placed into a manageable, organized structure that is recognizable by the ROOFER program. Once this has been done, then ROOFER can use the information to create valuable outputs for decision making.

During the Maintenance Stage

During the maintenance stage of using ROOFER, the goal of inventory is to keep the inventory current by recording additions to the inventory; roof section repair dates, and roof section replacements, and to upgrade the completeness of inventory properties entered into ROOFER as needed.

About User Roles
