Built-Up Roofing: Ponding

Definition:

Standing water is present or there is evidence of ponding by the presence of staining. Water which remains after 48 hr is considered ponded water.

Severity Levels:

Low - Ponding is rated low severity due to the maintenance problems associated with it.

Measurement:

Measure square feet of affected area.

Density:

x 100 = Problem Density

A = total area of ponding (sq ft)

B = total area of roof section being rated (sq ft)
