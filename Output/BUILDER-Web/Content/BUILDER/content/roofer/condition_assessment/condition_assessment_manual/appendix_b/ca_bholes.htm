Built-Up Roofing: Holes

Description:

A membrane hole is any visible opening which extends through all membrane layers. Holes can be of various sizes and shapes, and can be located anywhere on the roof surface.

Severity Levels:

High - All holes in the membrane are considered high severity due to their high leak potential.

Measurement:

Count the total number of holes in the membrane. If the distance between two holes is less than 1 ft., count them as one hole.

Density:

x 100 = Problem Density

A = number of membrane holes

B = total area of roof section being rated (sq ft)
