Order of Standards, Policies, and Policy Sequences

In order for SMS Sustainment Management System work plans to function correctly, standards, policies, and policy sequences must be created and implemented in the proper order.

1. Standards

Work configuration must start with standards. Standards are numbers that act as work triggers and inspection triggers.

2. Policies

Policies apply the standards to specific inventory attributes such as System type or RSL Remaining Service Life Remaining Service Life.

3. Policy Sequences

Policy sequences determine the priority order in which the policies are applied.
