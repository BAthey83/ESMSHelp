Select a Report

Note: This topic explains how to access reports from the toolbar at the top of the Content Area, not from the main toolbar.

BUILDERâ„¢ offers a number of standard reports that can be viewed, printed, or exported to electronic files of several formats. Reports can be viewed by activating the Reports button in the local toolbar. The Reports Selector window will appear.

To view a report:

  1. Activate either the Standard or Custom button, as applicable and available.
  2. Select the desired report from the dropdown list.

The selected report will launch in the Report Viewer window.

When you select the Reports button on a toolbar at a particular inventory level, the dropdown list includes only reports applicable to that level. Additionally, the report will only show data for the current mode you are in. For example, if the Reports button is clicked while in inspection mode, only inspection related reports will be displayed.

Tip: If the "Select a Report" field produces no dropdown list when you activate the down arrow, it could be because you are attempting to generate a report at too high a hierarchical level, such as at the Organization level in the inventory tree.
