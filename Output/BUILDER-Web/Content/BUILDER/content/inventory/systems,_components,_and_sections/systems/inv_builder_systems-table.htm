BUILDER Systems and Components

This topic presents the identifying numbers and names of the Systems and Components used in BUILDER.

To divide a Building into Systems and Components, BUILDER follows the Naval Facilities Engineering Command (NAVFAC) UNIFORMAT II classification for building elements (Level 2 and 3).*

*see https://www.wbdg.org/ffc/navy-navfac/design-build-request-proposal/uniformat-structure.
