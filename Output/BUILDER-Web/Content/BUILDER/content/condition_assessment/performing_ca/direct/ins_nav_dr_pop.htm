Navigating the Direct Rating Inspection Screen

The direct rating inspection screen has two tabs: Inspection and Attachments. Above the tabs is the toolbar.

Direct Rating Toolbar

The items on the direct rating toolbar are as follows:

  • Record Lock Indicator. The record lock indicator is located at the far left of the toolbar.
    1. Open Lock: If the lock shows as open (), the inspection is unlocked and can be edited if you have appropriate permissions.
    2. Closed lock: If the lock shows as closed (), the inspection is locked. For example, it may have been exported to BRED BUILDER Remote Entry Database (BuilderRED).
  • Save. Saves your inspection.
  • Comment. Allows you to add, edit, view, or delete comments pertaining to the inspection.
  • Reports. Launches the report tool.
  • Breadcrumbs. The breadcrumbs shown on the toolbar provide both orientation and an alternative method of navigation.

Inspection Tab

The Inspection tab is where you will enter inspection identification data and then select the appropriate color rating for the Component-Section in the Direct Condition Rating Scale box.

Attachments Tab

The Attachments tab is where you can view, add, or delete any attachments associated with the inspection.
