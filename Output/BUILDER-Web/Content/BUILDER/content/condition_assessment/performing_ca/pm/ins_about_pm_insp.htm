What Is a Preventative Maintenance Inspection?

In BUILDER, the PM inspection Preventative Maintenance InspectionPreventative maintenance inspection allows each subcomponent of a Component-Section to be separately evaluated either (a) according to criteria designated for that subcomponent, or (b) by a direct rating. An inspector has the option to "mix and match" the two approaches, (a) and (b), within the same PM inspection Preventative Maintenance InspectionPreventative maintenance inspection on a given Component-Section.

Another benefit, in addition to the flexibility to choose between the two evaluation options listed above, is that a PM inspection Preventative Maintenance InspectionPreventative maintenance inspection in BUILDER optionally allows for a predefined list of inspection tasks (such as "check for cracks") to be provided that each inspector is to follow for that subcomponent.

When to Use a Preventative Maintenance Inspection

PM Preventative MaintenancePreventative Maintenance inspections are done when called for by your company's procedures. Also, they can be useful when it's desirable to provide standardized checklists of inspection tasks (via the PM Preventative MaintenancePreventative Maintenance Task List) and/or inspection criteria.
