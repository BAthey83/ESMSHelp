Single-Ply Roofing: Splits

Definition:

Splits are cracks or tears that extend through the membrane. They vary in length from a few inches to the length of the roof and in width from hair-line to more than 1 in.

Severity Levels:

High - All splits in the membrane are considered high severity due to their leak potential.

Measurement:

Measure length of split.

Density:

x 100 = Problem Density

A = total length of membrane splits (ft).

B = total area or roof section being rated (sq ft).
