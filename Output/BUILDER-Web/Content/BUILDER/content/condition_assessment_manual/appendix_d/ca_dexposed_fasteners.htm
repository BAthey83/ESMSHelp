Shingle Roofing: Exposed Fasteners

Definition:

Shingle fasteners are visible in the field of the roof.

Notes:

If a shingle fastener has backed out, count it as a hole

Severity Levels:

Medium - A fastener is exposed but not backed out.

Measurement:

Measure the number of exposed fasteners. Individual exposed fasteners count as 1 sq ft minimum. If more than one exposed fastener is found in an area of 1 sq ft, count the distressed area as 1 sq ft.

Density:

x 100 = Problem Density

A = total area of exposed fasteners (sq ft) having defects.

B = total area of roof section being rated (sq ft).
