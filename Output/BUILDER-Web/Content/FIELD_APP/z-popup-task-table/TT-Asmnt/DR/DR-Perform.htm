Performing a Direct Rating

Operations you can perform on a direct rating are

  • View
  • Add
  • Edit
  • Delete

You can view a direct rating any time from Systems tab of the navigation pane. The navigation pane is viewable from any of the top tabs (except for the File tab at the left), as long as no subtask has been selected at the ribbon. If the navigation pane has been auto-hidden, bring it into view using the Navigation tab at the left of the screen.

 

Productivity Tip: For how to resize the navigation pane, or use Auto Hide to use limited screen space efficiently, see the topic Manipulating Navigation and Content Panes.

View a Direct Rating

To view a direct rating,

  1. Select any one of the top tabs except for the File tab.
  2. Mark the "Direct Rating" checkbox in the navigation pane toolbar. A dropdown field will appear. {{afdo - show screenshot with first entry of the dropdown. Redbox it.}}
  3. Single-click the desired rating date in the dropdown list. This results in the Direct Rating column showing the assessment status of assets as of that date. {{ screenshot. }}

Add a Direct Rating

To add a direct rating,

  1. Select the assessment plans page.
  2. Activate Direct Rating on the ribbon.
  3. In the "Direct Rating" popup, activate the Add New button. {{afdo - "result" style:}} The current date and time should appear in the Direct Rating dropdown field on the navigation pane toolbar.
  4. Navigate to the first component to be rated.
  5. Single-click on the entry in the component's "Direct Rating" column. (This will typically be "0 - No Rating".) The table of color ratings will appear. {{afdo - screenshot}}
  6. Click the appropriate color rating. For color rating guidelines, see {{afdo include appropriate appendix and linke it here}}, or for paint rating guidelines see {{link}}.
  7. To rate an additional component, navigate to it and repeat steps 5 through 7 until finished.

Edit a Direct Rating

To change an existing direct rating at the navigation pane, if you have appropriate permission and are within the editing window, do the following:

  1. In the navigation pane toolbar, mark the "Direct Rating" checkbox.
  2. Select the date of the rating you wish to edit. (for more detailed directions, see {{afdo - link view a direct rating}}.
  3. Navigate to the component in the asset tree.
  4. Single-click on the entry in the component's "Direct Rating" column. The table of color ratings will appear. {{.afdo - "result" style}}
  5. Click the appropriate color rating. For color rating guidelines, see {{afdo include appropriate appendix and linke it here}}, or for paint rating guidelines see {{link}}.
  6. To edit an additional component, navigate to it and repeat steps 4 through 6 until finished.

Delete a Direct Rating

To delete a direct rating,

  1. Select the assessment plans page.
  2. Activate Direct Rating on the ribbon.
  3. In the "Direct Rating" popup, navigate in the Direct Rating List field to the assessment date to be deleted.
  4. After selecting the assessment date, activate the Delete button.

Note: you may only delete a rating you have created, not one that someone else created, unless you are at the $$ permissions level or above.
