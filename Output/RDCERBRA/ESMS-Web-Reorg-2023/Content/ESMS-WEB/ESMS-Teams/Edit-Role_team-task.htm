Edit Role (Security Permissions)

To edit a role,

  1. At the Team Editor screen, select the Roles tab.
  2. Activate the desired role in the list of roles. The role name and a list of its permission settings will appear under "Role Details" at the right.
  1. Check and uncheck permission options until you have the desired combination.
  2. Activate the Save button. A confirmation message should appear.
