Delete a Team Member

This task requires that you have permission to administer a team.

To delete a team member from a team,

  1. At the Team Editor screen, select the Teams tab.
  2. In the Team Details content area, navigate to the "Team Members" block.
  3. Activate the Remove button next to the member to be deleted from the team.

Caution: No confirmation popup is presented when deleting a team member from a team.
