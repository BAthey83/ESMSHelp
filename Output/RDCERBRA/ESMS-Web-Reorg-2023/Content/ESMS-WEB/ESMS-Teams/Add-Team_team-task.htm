Add Team at the Teams Tab

To add a team,

  1. At the Team Editor screen, select the Teams tab.
  2. Activate the "+" button in the colored bar at the bottom of the screen.

  3. In the "Create Team" popup, enter a name for the new team in the "Team Name" field.
  4. To confirm, activate the Create button.

  5. Alternatively, activate the Cancel button to cancel adding the team.

Add Team Members

Adding team members is explained in the topic Add Team Members. If you're unfamiliar with the layout of the Team Details display, you might want first to refer to "Navigating Team Details" in the topic Understanding the Teams Screen.

Assign Permission Roles to the Team

Assigning site and/or group permission roles to the team is explained in the topic Assign a Permissions Role. If you're unfamiliar with the layout of the Role Details display, you might want first to refer to "Navigating Role Details" in the topic Understanding the Teams Screen.
