Organization Details Screen

This screen shows the Organization information. It is accessible from the main menu or from the ESMS Dashboard.

Organization Search:

You can look for Organizations using the Search screen shown above. Type in the Organization, or select one from the resulting menu.

Organization Options include:

  • Air Force
  • Defense Health Agency
  • Department of Defense
  • Army Active
