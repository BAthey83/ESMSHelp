Select Site(s) for Work Item Generation

This topic shows how to select one or more target sites for generation of work items. This selection may be made either before or after setting standards, but must be done before generating the work items.

Two entry fields, located in the left half of the "Work Generation" screen, assist in target site(s) selection:

  • "Search Sites" is where you search for and select one or more sites.
  • "Selected Sites" is where your selections are recorded.

Add or Delete Sites from the "Selected Sites" List

Add Site(s)

To select a site,

  1. Type into the "Search Sites" field one of the following:
    1. The site name, or
    2. A portion of the site name as a search term

    A dropdown list of sites matching the search term will appear.

  2. Click one of the results shown in the dropdown list.It will be added to the list shown in the "Selected Sites" field.
  3. If you want to select an additional site, repeat the first two steps.

Delete a Site

To remove a site from the list of selected sites, activate the Delete button to the left of the site name.

Note: The Delete button is a white "x" on a square blue background.
