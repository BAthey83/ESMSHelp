Charter Stage

This topic describes the Charter stage of developing an ESMS assessment.

After you have Add Assessment Planson page 1, you can start the process of designing an assessment plan. There are five stages to be navigated in the development process, with transitions between the steps that may require approvals. The stages are listed in Understanding Assessment Planson page 1. The transitions are guided by the user-defined Schemeon page 1.

—Quick Links—

Tasks at this stage are to

  • enter or edit assessment plan details, at the Details Tabon page 1
  • view read-only assessment plan information that was set during assessment plan creation, but can also be viewed at the Details Tabon page 1
  • select assets to be assessed, at the Assets Tabon page 1
  • select groups to work with, at the Groups Tabon page 1
  • select planning team members, at the Operator Pre-Assessment Team Tabon page 1
  • Transitionon page 1 to the next stage when done.

Transition

When entries are complete and any required approvals have been obtained (this is governed by the scheme; see Transitionson page 1 in Understanding Assessment Planson page 1), you can proceed to the next stage, Pre-Assessment stageon page 1.
