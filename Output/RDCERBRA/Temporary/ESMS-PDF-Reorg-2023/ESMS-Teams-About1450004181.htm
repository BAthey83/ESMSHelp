Understanding the Teams Screen

This topic introduces tasks you can perform at the Teams screen and provides links to greater detail for each. These tasks require that you have permission to administer a team. If you don't have permission to administer a team, you can still view your team associations at the Enterprise SMS Dashboard.

What Can I Do Here?

At the Teams tab on the Teams screen you can

  • add and delete teams
  • add and delete team members
  • assign roles (security permissions) to teams
  • rename teams

At the Roles tab you can

  • add and delete customized security permissions roles
  • edit roles
  • rename roles

Navigating the Team Screen

At the Team screen, you can select the Teams tab to view Navigating Team Detailson page 1, or the Roles tab to view Navigating Role Detailson page 1.

Across the bottom of the screen is a blue command bar. Icons on the command bar provide you with additional action options. These options will differ depending on whether you are at the Teams tab or the Roles tab. Active icons show explanatory labels when you hover over them, and will be explained where they are used in connection with team tasks.

Navigating Team Details

When you select a team from the list at the Teams tab, Team Details appear in the content pane at the right. This is what the Details of a newly created team will look like before any changes:

The "Team Members" area lists the current membership of the team. When you extend team membership invitations at the "Invite Links" area. the new team members' names will appear in the "Team Members" area after they accept the invitations.

The "Team Workspace" area is where the team leader assigns site and group permissions roles to the team as a whole. Creation and design of permissions roles takes place at the Roles tab.
