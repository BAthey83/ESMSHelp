Delete Role (Security Permissions)

This topic explains deleting a security permissions role in Enterprise SMS Web. This task requires that you have permission to administer a team.

To delete a role,

  1. At the Team Editor screen, select the Roles tab.
  2. Activate the desired role in the list of roles.
  3. Activate the "X" button in the toolbar below.

WARNING: There will be no confirmation step.

  1. Activate OK in the box that reports deletion of the role.
