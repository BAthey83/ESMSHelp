Understanding the Inventory Screen

This topic introduces tasks you can perform at the Inventory screen and provides links to greater detail for each.

What Can I Do Here?

The Inventory screen is where you search for organizations, facilities, groups, and components, applying filters if desired and narrowing your search to applicable sites and domains. You can also configure the resulting data by selecting the information fields of interest to you.

Note: Before you begin a search, you must select at least one domain from the main menu bar dropdown. Selecting a site is optional (all sites permitted to you are active if you don't select a site).

Once the results are delivered to the screen, you can access the detail pages for the resulting organizations, facilities, groups, or components.

Alternatively, you can just begin at the starting page (which shows facilities by default) and navigate/drill down to the desired information.

Inventory Screen Information

A great deal of information is available via the Inventory screen once you have results and display the corresponding detail pages:

Organization Details

At the Organization Details Screenon page 1 page you can see

  • a list of organizations with general information such as associated sites and performance records
  • funding information for each organization
  • a list of organization teams
  • a list of organization roles
  • a list of organization members
  • a list of reference books related to the organization

Facility Details

At the Facility Detailson page 1 page you can see

  • a list of components in the facility
  • a list of assessment plans for all components in the facility
  • a list of work items for all components in the facility

Group Details

At the Group Detailson page 1 page you can see

  • what components are in the group, if any
  • what child groups are in the group, if any
  • group associations
  • a list of assessment plans for all components contained in the group or its child groups
  • a list of work items for all components contained in the group or its child groups

Component Details

At the Component Detailson page 1 page you can see

  • component attributes
  • a list of assessment plans performed on the component
  • a list of work items associated with the component
  • the component's inspection schedule
  • associated documents, such as images

As mentioned at Inventory Screen Taskson page 1, you can also upload documents, which will be automatically associated with that component.
