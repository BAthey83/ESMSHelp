Add a Service Life Book

This topic explains how to create a custom service life book. The topic Edit a Service Life Bookon page 1 data explains how to add or edit data in a custom service life book.

Permissions Note: Adding a custom service life book requires Work Planner permission level or above at the target Organization.

To add a custom service life book,

  1. At the main menu, select the Reference Books drop-down, followed by the Service Life button. The Service Life menu appears.
  2. Select the "+" button to add a new Service Life book.
  3. Edit the Service Life data as needed.

To see how to edit data for a Service Life book, go to the Edit a Service Life Bookon page 1 topic.
