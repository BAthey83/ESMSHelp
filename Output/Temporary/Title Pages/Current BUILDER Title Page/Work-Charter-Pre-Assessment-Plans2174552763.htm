Pre-Assessment Plan Team Tab

Assessors selected for the Pre-Assessment Plan stage are listed at the Pre-Assessment Plan Team tab. Here you can add or remove team members from the list, and assign each of them to one or more appropriate disciplines (Civil, Electrical, Structural, etc.)

Add Assessor to the Team List

To add an assessor to the pre-assessment plan team,

  1. Activate the Add button. The "User Search" popup will appear.
  2. Enter search text in the Name field at the top of the popup, then activate the Go button at the right side of the field.

Note: You will get all results where the search text appears in the user name, even if it is in the middle or at the end of the user name.

  1. If too many results appear, or if no results appear, you may want to change the search text and try again.
  2. Once the list appearing in the "User Search" popup is sufficiently filtered, select one user name from the filtered list. You can only select one name per Add activation.
  3. To finalize the selection, activate the Select button at the bottom of the popup. (Alternatively, activate Close to cancel your selection.) You will be returned to the Pre-Assessment Plan Team tab.

Edit Assignment of Disciplines

To assign an assessor to one or more disciplines (Civil, Electrical, Structural, etc.),

  1. Activate the Manage Disciplines button in the row for the assessor. The "Manage Disciplines" popup will appear.
  2. Mark the checkbox for each desired discipline.
  3. To unmark a checked box, activate the box again.
  4. When you are satisfied with your selection, exit the "Manage Disciplines" popup. The selection will be preserved.

Remove Assessor from the Team List

To remove an assessor from the list at the Pre-Assessment Plan Team tab, activate the Remove button in the corresponding line of the table.
