Review Stage

This topic describes the Review stage of designing an ESMS assessment plan. The idea is that here, a different team can check over the information before synchronizing to ESMS Desktop in preparation for the assessment plan. This is the third of five stages, to be performed after approval has been obtained to progress from the Pre-Assessment Plan stageon page 1 stage. After you complete the Review stage, there will be tasks to be performed outside of ESMS Web in the Desktop Sync / Assessment Stageon page 1.

— Quick Links —

The order of tasks in this stage is essential. Tasks at this stage are to

  1. Review Tabs on page 1 contained at the Charter, Assets, Groups, Documents, Assessor and Operator Pre-Assessment Teams, Assessor and Operator Questionnaire, and Assessment Team tabs.
  2. Make Changes as Neededon page 1. You will need to navigate to previous stages to do so.
  3. Transitionon page 1 to the next stage when done.

Review Tabs

Note that the tabs described below are all for review only. No changes to the procedure can be made at the Review stage.

  • Charter (Read-Only). At this tab, you can review (but not change) the charter, planned assessment date, Site, etc.
  • Assets (Read-Only). At this tab, you can review (but not change) what assets were selected for assessment plans.
  • Groups (Read-Only). At this tab, you can review (but not change) what groups were selected for assessment plans.
  • Documents (Read-Only). At this tab, you can review (but not change) the documents that are associated with the workflow.
  • Assessor and Operator Pre-Assessment Teams (Read-Only). At this tab, you can review (but not change) the makeup of (1) the assessor pre-assessment plan team and (2) the operator assessment plan team.
  • Assessor and Operator Questionnaires (Read-Only). At this tab, you can review (but not change) the assessor and operator questionnaires for the assessment plan.
  • Assessment Team (Read-Only). At this tab, you can review, (but not change), the makeup of the assessment plan team.

Make Changes as Needed

To make any changes, you will need to navigate to the appropriate earlier stages in the workflow.

Transition

When review and edits are complete and any required approvals have been obtained (this is governed by the scheme; see Transitions in Understanding Assessment Planson page 1), you must (1) synchronize, (2) perform the assessment plan on desktop (from field notes) or tablet, and (3) re-synchronize after the assessment plan before proceeding to the next stage, the Post-Assessment Plan Stageon page 1.
