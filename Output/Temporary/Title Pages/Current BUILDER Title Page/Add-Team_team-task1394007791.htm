Add a Team

This topic will explain establishing a new team in Enterprise SMS Web, and provides instructions for the first major step in the process.This task requires that you have permission to administer a team.

To set up a team you will want to follow these major steps, the first of which is described in this topic:

  • Add Team at the Teams Tabon page 1
  • Add Team Memberson page 1
  • Assign Permission Roles to the Teamon page 1

Note: If you're unfamiliar with the layout of the Team Details and/or Role Details display at the Teams ("Team Editor") screen, you might want first to refer to "Navigating the Team Screen" in the topic Understanding the Teams Screenon page 1 before implementing the second and third major steps listed above.

Add Team at the Teams Tab

To add a team,

  1. At the Team Editor screen, select the Teams tab.
  2. Activate the "+" button in the colored bar at the bottom of the screen.

  3. In the "Create Team" popup, enter a name for the new team in the "Team Name" field.
  4. To confirm, activate the Create button.

  5. Alternatively, activate the Cancel button to cancel adding the team.
